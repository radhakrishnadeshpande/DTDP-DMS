Digital Techniques for Design and Planning

B.TECH (DTDP) is a full time degree program in Digital Techniques for Design and Planning. It is a multi-disciplinary course, which relates to DESIGN, MULTIMEDIA, GlS, and IT. A strong foundation of multidisciplinary skills related to space, design, construction, environment, multimedia, geographical information systems and information technology are offered to the students.
